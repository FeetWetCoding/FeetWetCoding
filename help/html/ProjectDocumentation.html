A Note About This Documentation


This documentation is currently generated with the helpndoc app.  We are currently working on getting a collaborative-based site up on feetwetcoding.com.  Anyone who would like to help us with the site would be welcome!  Contributors will need these qualities:


  • Patient, friendly, and even-tempered.
  • Able to work well with others.
  • Willing to follow the standards and editorial policies of this site.


Anyone who joins us and helps out will have our Undying GratitudeTM.  Active contributors will be publicly recognized for their work, have a bit of Authoritah, and will also receive a small gift after they have Proven Themselves Worthy (like a FeetWetCoding.com coffee mug or T-Shirt or something.  :-)


Plus, people would think you were Teh Cool, and surely goodness and mercy would follow you all the days of your life, and stuff!
