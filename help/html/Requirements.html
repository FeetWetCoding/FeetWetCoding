What you need in order to use our project to learn C++:


o A Windows, Mac or Linux computer that can display a screen resolution of at least 1024x768.

o The Qt SDK including the Qt Creator IDE.

o The FeetWetCoding software itself.

o Some form of C++ educational reference (such as a C++ textbook, classroom instruction or even http://www.cplusplus.com/doc/tutorial/ )

o A desire to learn C++.

o The willingness to do the work.
